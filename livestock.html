Saltwater Fish

From clowns and tangs to wrasses and gobies. Ask us about compatibility and tank size before purchasing.

  • Acclimation recommended; see Care Guides.
  • Quarantine when possible; add peaceful fish first, territorial fish last.
  • Bring a water sample or book a test for best results.

Freshwater Fish

Community fish, cichlids, shrimp-safe options, and more. We’ll help plan a peaceful mix.

  • Cycle your tank first; see Cycling.
  • Match species for temperament and water parameters.
Shop Freshwater Fish

Corals

Softies, LPS, SPS, frags & colonies. Lighting and flow matter—ask us to help place new corals.

  • Target light ranges: Soft 50–100 PAR • LPS 75–150 • SPS 200–350 (see Lighting).
  • Keep alkalinity, calcium, magnesium stable (see Parameters).
Shop Corals

Inverts — Saltwater

Clean-up crew, shrimp, crabs, snails, urchins, starfish and more. Great for algae and detritus control.

  • Check compatibility with fish (some crabs/shrimps are opportunistic).
  • Acclimate slowly; many inverts are sensitive to salinity swings.
Shop SW Inverts

Inverts — Freshwater

Shrimp, snails and more for planted and community tanks. Watch copper-based meds around inverts.

  • Keep stable parameters; avoid sudden pH/TDS shifts.
Shop FW Inverts

Freshwater Plants

Anubias, Java fern, stem plants, mosses, carpeting species and more. Ask about nutrient dosing and CO₂.

  • Match light and substrate to plant type; dose macros/micros as needed.
  • Great for stability and natural algae control.
Shop FW Plants

Notes & Policies

  • Availability: Live inventory changes quickly; call ahead or visit for current stock. Special orders available.
  • Health: We strive to sell only healthy livestock; please acclimate carefully and avoid mixing aggressive species.
  • DOA/Returns: See in-store policy; contact us immediately with concerns at pickup.
