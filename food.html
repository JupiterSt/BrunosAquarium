Frozen Fish Food

High-protein blends for growth and body condition. Rotate varieties to cover herbivore, omnivore, and carnivore needs.

  • Great for picky eaters; thaw in tank water and target feed when needed.
  • Remove uneaten food to keep nutrients in check.

Frozen Coral Food

Particle sizes suited for LPS and larger polyps; enhance polyp extension and coloration.

  • Target feed with flow turned down; avoid overfeeding.

Pellets — Fish

Clean, shelf-stable staples. Choose pellet size for species and mix formulas for variety.

  • Soak briefly if buoyancy causes gulping; feed small portions 1–2× daily.

Pellets — Corals

Sized for LPS target feeding and broadcast feeding mixes.

  • Turn down flow and use a feeding pipette; rinse filters if needed.

Live Food (Pods & Phyto)

Excellent for finicky fish and filter feeders. Live copepods support mandarins and wrasses; phytoplankton feeds corals and boosts microfauna.

  • Seed refugiums and rockwork with pods; dose phyto regularly for stability.
  • Refrigerate per label and check expiration dates.
