Filtration

Hang‑on‑back, canisters, sumps, skimmers, media reactors, and biological media to keep water crystal clear.

  • Match filter flow to tank size; aim for 4–10× turnover, higher for reefs.
  • Use quality bio‑media and rinse mechanical media regularly.

Heating & Lighting

  • Use controllers for heater safety; keep spares for emergencies.
  • Pick lighting by livestock needs (see Reef Lighting).
